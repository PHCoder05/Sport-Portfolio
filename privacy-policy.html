Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 22, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Capriole Sports Tech Private Limited. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure environment for using our Capriole Admin Mobile App (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide:

We may collect personal information that you provide when using the App, including but not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Account information
  • Profile photo
  • Details of your sports facilities (location and photos)
  • Booking history
  • Customer payment information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information:

We may also collect certain information automatically when you use the App, including:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system)
  • Usage information (e.g., app interactions, pages visited)
  • Location information (with your consent)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

3.1 Providing Services:

To provide venue booking services, process bookings, and facilitate communication between users and venue providers.

3.2 Customer Support:

To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

3.3 Improving Our Services:

To analyze usage patterns, perform research, and enhance our App's functionality.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With End Users (Sports Enthusiasts):

We may share details of your sports venue / academy with end users to facilitate reservation of your venue or avail coaching from your academy.

4.2 With Service Providers:

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App and providing services.

4.3 Legal Compliance:

We may share information as required by law, court order, or government regulations.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its use. You can update your information within the App or contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us, and we will promptly remove it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or by other means.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: admin@capriolesportstech.com

10. Consent

By using our Capriole Admin Mobile App, you consent to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
