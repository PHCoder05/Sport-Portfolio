Pricing and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1-10-2023

Thank you for choosing Capriole for your sport venue booking and academy management needs. We are committed to providing a seamless and convenient experience for all sports lovers. Please take a moment to review our pricing and refund policy below.

Pricing

1. Subscription Plans

  • We offer various subscription plans to cater to different needs. These plans may include features such as venue booking, academy management tools, and more.
  • Pricing details for each subscription plan can be found on our website. We reserve the right to change our pricing at any time but will notify you in advance of any changes.
  • Your subscription will be billed on a [monthly/annual] basis, and you will be charged the applicable fee in advance.

2. Additional Services

  • Some services or features may be offered separately from our subscription plans. The pricing for these services will be clearly outlined on our website.
  • Charges for additional services will be billed separately and may be subject to change.

3. Payment Methods

  • We accept payment via [list accepted payment methods, e.g., credit cards, UPI]. You are responsible for providing accurate billing information.
  • By using our services, you authorize us to charge your chosen payment method for all fees and charges incurred.

4. Taxes

  • Applicable taxes, if any, will be added to your subscription fee or additional service charges.

Refund Policy

1. Subscription Refunds

  • We offer a [30/60/90]-day money-back guarantee for new subscription sign-ups. If you are not satisfied with our services within this period, you may request a full refund.
  • After the money-back guarantee period has passed, subscription fees are non-refundable.

2. Cancellation of Subscription

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time by visiting your account settings on our website. Upon cancellation, you will not be billed for future subscription periods.
  • No refunds will be issued for the remaining days of the current billing cycle.

3. Refunds for Additional Services

  • Refunds for additional services will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our customer support team to discuss your situation.

4. Refund Process

  • To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at admin@capriolesportstech.com. Be sure to provide your account details and the reason for your refund request.
  • Refunds, if approved, will be processed within 7 business days and will be credited to the original payment method.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our pricing and refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at admin@capriolesportstech.com. We are here to assist you and ensure your experience with Capriole is a positive one.

Note: This pricing and refund policy is subject to change, and the most recent version will be posted on our website. Please review this policy periodically for updates.
